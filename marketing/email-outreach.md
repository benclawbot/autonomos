## Quick Tips for Email Outreach

✅ **Personalize** — Add 1-2 specific details about the recipient
✅ **Keep it short** — Under 200 words max
✅ **One clear CTA** — Reply, sign up, or book a call
✅ **Follow up** — Wait 3-4 days, then send a light follow-up
✅ **Track** — Use unique links to see who's clicking
