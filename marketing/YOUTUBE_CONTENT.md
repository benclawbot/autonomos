## Production Tips

### Recording
- Use Loom or OBS for screen capture
- Keep under 90 seconds for optimal retention
- Add captions - 80% watch on mute

### Thumbnail Ideas
- Bold text, high contrast
- Face + screen capture combo
- Numbers and arrows work well

### Posting Times (Optimal)
- 9AM EST - Morning scroll
- 12PM EST - Lunch break
- 6PM EST - Evening wind-down
- 9PM EST - Late-night scroll
